Why TintEd?

Today’s recruitment industry is trying every possible way to get best hire, but still they are unable to find desired candidates. Meanwhile, humans excel where machines falter: creating deep connections, observing and interpreting candidates’ behavior, and making decisions an empowering humans with AI can yield better results than he can accomplish alone.Strategic use of artificial intelligence, combined with the personal touch of human recruiters can help companies fill open positions faster and with more qualified candidates.
